Creamy tomato chicken pasta

Description

For a delicious creamy tomato chicken pasta, start by cooking 300 grams of your favorite pasta according to package instructions. In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat. Add 2 diced chicken breasts, season with salt and pepper, and cook until browned and fully cooked. Remove the chicken and set aside. In the same skillet, saut√© 1 finely chopped onion and 3 minced garlic cloves until fragrant and softened. Stir in 1 can of diced tomatoes and 1 tablespoon of tomato paste, cooking until the mixture thickens. Add 1 teaspoon of dried basil and 1 teaspoon of dried oregano for added flavor.

Reduce the heat and pour in 1 cup of heavy cream, stirring to combine. Let the sauce simmer for a few minutes until it becomes rich and creamy. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and mix well. Drain the pasta and toss it into the sauce, ensuring everything is well coated. For a final touch, stir in 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese until melted. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired. Serve hot and enjoy your creamy tomato chicken pasta!

Ingredients

  • 300 grams of pasta
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 diced chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 finely chopped onion
  • 3 minced garlic cloves
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish
