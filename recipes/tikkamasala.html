Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

Chicken Tikka Masala is a classic Indian dish that is rich, creamy, and packed with flavor. To make this dish, start by marinating 500 grams of chicken breast in a mixture of yogurt, lemon juice, and a blend of spices such as garam masala, cumin, and paprika. Let the chicken marinate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to develop.

Once the chicken is marinated, grill or bake it until it is cooked through and has a nice charred exterior. In a separate pan, saut√© a finely chopped onion, garlic, and ginger until fragrant and softened. Add a can of diced tomatoes, tomato paste, and a blend of spices such as garam masala, cumin, and paprika to create a rich and flavorful sauce.

Once the sauce has thickened, add the cooked chicken to the pan and simmer until the flavors meld together. Finish the dish by stirring in a cup of heavy cream to create a luscious and creamy sauce. Serve the Chicken Tikka Masala over steamed rice or with a side of naan bread for a delicious and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup of yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons of garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 finely chopped onion
  • 3 minced garlic cloves
  • 1 tablespoon of grated ginger
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato paste
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • Steamed rice or naan bread for serving
